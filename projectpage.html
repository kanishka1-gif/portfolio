News Aggregator

A news aggregator website collects news articles, blog posts, or other media content from various sources and presents them in one place. These websites help users stay updated on topics of interest without visiting multiple news sites..
Technologies used: Java, html,css and javascript.
In my news aggregator project, I use APIs to fetch the latest news articles from multiple sources dynamically. APIs allow my application to access real-time data from news providers without manually updating the content..
